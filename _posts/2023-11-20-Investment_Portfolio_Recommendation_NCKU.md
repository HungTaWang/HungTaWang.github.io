> Achieving 59% accuracy in predicting real-world market trends using a hybrid AHP and PCA model.

![First]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/res/First.png){:height="50%" width="50%"}

During my undergraduate studies at NCKU, I led a project to build an intelligent investment recommendation system. The goal was to provide users with data-driven portfolio advice through a convenient LINE Bot interface.

Project Architecture:

- **Data Integration**: Extracted financial data from the TEJ (Taiwan Economic Journal) database and managed it using PostgreSQL.
- **Feature Selection**: Used Principal Component Analysis (PCA) to reduce data dimensionality and identify the most significant market indicators.
- **Decision Engine**: Employed the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) to weigh different investment criteria and predict market movements.
- **User Interface**: Developed a LINE Bot that allows users to receive real-time recommendations and interact with the prediction model.

The system demonstrated a robust 59% accuracy rate in predicting ups and downs in the real-world market, highlighting the effectiveness of combining statistical analysis with structured decision-making frameworks.
