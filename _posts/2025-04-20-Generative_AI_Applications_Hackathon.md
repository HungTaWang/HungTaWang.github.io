> Developing an Agent-based Patient Care System using AWS and Generative AI.

![Post]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/res/GenAI.png){:height="100%" width="100%"}

**提案主旨: 長者照護之智慧情緒感知系統**

隨著全球高齡化加劇，長照需求日益提升，情緒健康已成為影響長者身心狀態的關鍵因素。然而，現有的照護框架受限於人力資源不足，照護人員難以即時掌握每位長者的情緒變化，進而增加潛在的健康風險。

以下為本計畫之核心貢獻：

- 計畫價值： 本計畫整合 ICAM 邊緣影像技術（Edge AI）與 AWS 生成式 AI，開發出一套智慧情緒感知系統。該系統能即時偵測長者表情（如：開心、憂鬱），並生成具備溫暖度與針對性的語音回應，提供即時情緒安撫並提升照護品質。
- 實作與擴展性： 系統能有效降低人力負荷並強化服務差異化。未來，此架構可擴展至居家及社區照護場域，並結合健康管理平台進行長期的預測性分析（Predictive Analytics）。

**應用價值與結論**

本系統成功將情緒數據轉化為可操作的照護資訊，具備高度商業化潛力。其目標在於建立智慧長照的新標準，為未來高齡化社會的挑戰提供具備韌性且技術驅動的解決方案。

---

**Core Focus: Intelligent Emotion Sensing System for Elderly Care**

As global aging intensifies, the demand for long-term care continues to escalate, with emotional health emerging as a critical factor influencing the physical and mental well-being of the elderly. Current caregiving frameworks are often constrained by human resource limitations, making it difficult for personnel to monitor real-time emotional fluctuations, thereby increasing potential health risks.

The core contributions of this project are as follows:

- Project Value: By integrating ICAM Edge AI imaging technology with AWS Generative AI, this project develops an intelligent emotion sensing system. It enables the real-time detection of elderly facial expressions (e.g., happiness, distress) and generates warm, targeted voice responses to provide emotional soothing and enhance care quality.
- Implementation & Scalability: This system effectively reduces the burden on human resources and strengthens service differentiation. Looking forward, the architecture is designed for expansion into home and community care settings, with the potential to integrate with health management platforms for long-term predictive analytics.

**Actionable Value & Conclusion**

The proposed system transforms emotional data into actionable care insights, possessing high commercialization potential. It aims to establish a new standard for smart long-term care, providing a robust, technology-driven solution for the aging population challenges of the future.
