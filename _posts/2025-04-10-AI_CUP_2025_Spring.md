> Bronze Medalist in the AI CUP 2025 Spring (Ranked 3rd out of 633 teams nationwide).

![Ranking]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/res/Ranking.png){:height="50%" width="50%"}

In this competition hosted by the Ministry of Education, our team was tasked with analyzing sensor data from smart table tennis rackets to classify different types of strokes and movements.

Our approach involved:

- **Feature Engineering**: Converted raw waveform data into spectrograms using Fourier Transforms to capture frequency-domain features.
- **Hybrid Modeling**: Developed a sophisticated model combining LightGBM for tabular data features and CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks) for the generated spectrograms.
- **Addressing Imbalance**: Implemented strategies to handle the class imbalance in the dataset, ensuring the model performed well across both common and rare movement types.

The resulting model achieved a private leaderboard score of 0.835080, securing us a spot on the national podium.
