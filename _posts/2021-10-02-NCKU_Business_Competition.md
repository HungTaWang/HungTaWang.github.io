> Team Leader of the champion team in the NCKU Business Competition.

<p>在參加成功盃個案分析競賽期間，我在討論時被 EMBA 學姊評為反應敏捷且擅長應對變化。因此，我負責帶領討論、總結陳述以及評審問答環節。這些任務都要求我根據當下狀況進行即時調整，亟需快速反應的能力，而這一切都透過平時的準備以及密集的討論得到了進步。</p>

<p>我們的專案核心圍繞著 AKB48 該如何擴展海外市場，我們需要在台灣、中國、韓國、印尼、泰國以及菲律賓六國之中選擇一個國家拓展，除了要有創新的行銷方案之外，還要有足夠的市場調查與數據分析來支持我們的論點。此外，我們還需要考慮到不同國家的文化差異，並提出符合當地市場需求的解決方案。最後，我們同時也認為應該要發揮所學，加入相較其他組更為縝密的財務規劃。</p>

<p>儘管理想是完整的，但實際執行時卻還是有許多問題，例如實際的成本應該是哪些數額，資本支出率該如何定義，抑或是投資人最關心的獲利成長又該如何預估，這些都使得我們在合作以及討論時產生了許多分歧，最終我們透過分工合作，先保證有一定的成果，再根據不同成員的專長進行調整。即使這樣的模式在當時還是耗費了許多時間在意見整合上，但這也使得我們的提案更完整，也更全面。</p>

<p>過程中無數次的修改與質疑，幫助我們在最後評審提問時，雖然被問到了意料之外的難題，但我們還是能夠沉著應對，並給出能使評審滿意的回答，也因為我們的合作無間，最終我們獲得了冠軍！</p>

---

<p>During my participation in the NCKU Case Study Competition, I was recognized by an EMBA mentor for my quick thinking and adaptability during our discussions. As a result, I was tasked with leading the team discussions, delivering the final presentation, and handling the judges' Q&A session. These responsibilities required me to make real-time adjustments based on the situation, demanding rapid response skills that I continuously improved through thorough preparation and intensive group deliberations.</p>

<p>Our project focused on strategic market expansion for AKB48 into overseas markets. We had to select one target country from among Taiwan, China, South Korea, Indonesia, Thailand, and the Philippines. Beyond developing innovative marketing schemes, we needed to support our arguments with robust market research and data analysis. Furthermore, we had to account for cultural differences and propose solutions tailored to local market demands. Finally, we decided to leverage our academic background by incorporating a financial plan that was significantly more rigorous than those of our competitors.</p>

<p>While our vision was comprehensive, the execution phase presented numerous challenges, such as determining actual cost figures, defining capital expenditure (CAPEX) rates, and forecasting earnings growth—a primary concern for investors. These complexities led to several disagreements during our collaborations. We resolved this by first ensuring a baseline result through shared labor, then refining the project based on each member's specific expertise. Although this iterative process required significant time for consensus-building, it ultimately made our proposal more complete and well-rounded.</p>

<p>The countless revisions and internal critiques we underwent prepared us for the final Q&A. Despite being faced with unexpected and challenging questions from the judges, we remained composed and provided satisfying answers. Thanks to our seamless teamwork, we ultimately won the championship!</p>
